FOLKLORE TRAVELER

Experience culture, differently.

Enjoy Romania's rural beauty

When visiting the country side, checking in to a Hotel, although it provides you with modern comfort, will take away from your experience. We at “Folklore Traveler” believe that for one to appreciate the simple rural life you have to be a part of it. With this in mind we offer you the chance to live in a traditional homestead during your stay, interact with the towns folk and learn about the different traditions and customs of that region.
